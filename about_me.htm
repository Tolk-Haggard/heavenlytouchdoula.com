My name is Casey Haggard and I am the proud owner of Heavenly Touch Doula Services. I am a mother of 8 children and have been married to my husband Brian for 16 years. Brian and I are high school sweethearts and we had our first baby when we were 16 years old.

Though I was very young, my love and interest for childbirth started my journey. I worked in labor and delivery as an EMT 14 years ago. At that time I decided to stop working and take care and homeschool our children. Since having my last baby Madison, I decided to continue my journey.

I have since started training through Childbirth International as a birth Doula. My hope is to start my working towards my RN and eventually get my Midwife degree.

My philosophy on childbirth is that I know that a woman needs to feel emotionally and physically supported during her labor, birth and postpartum period. During this glorious time you have a beautiful story to write. This is your time, your story! You can rest assured in knowing that I will help you in achieving the birth you want!

Relevant volunteer or advocacy work: I have 8 children. My first two were natural. The other 6 were c-sections.
