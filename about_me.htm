Fee Details: My fee includes two prenatal visits, unlimited email, phone or text. I will provide constant care for you through out your labor and birth. If you choose, I offer 2 postpartum visits in the cost. I also offer separate postpartum care. During my visit I can cook, clean, do laundry, help with breastfeeding and newborn care. Though I am not certified in breastfeeding I have breastfed my children and are quite knowledgeable in this area. I have 8 children ages 17, 14, 11, 10, 6, 4, 3, and 15 months. I also worked in the Emergency room and Labor and Delivery as an EMT. I work very well with doctors and nurses. I am currently working on my certification through Childbirth International and in the new year I will be working on my midwife assistant through Birth Arts International. Techniques I use are breathing, massage, birthing ball (upon request), rebozo, and position change. Feel free to contact me for an interview. I look forward to meeting and working with you!
