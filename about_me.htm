I am a mother of 8 kids and I have been married to my husband Brian for 16 years. I am certifying through Childbirth International and hope to start midwifery training sometime in 2016.

I became interested in birth after I became a mother. I feel that motherhood is such a beautiful and strong bond. I will work with you so that you can have the birth you want!

My qualifications are:

Birth Doula Experience: 1 year

Postpartum Doula Experience: 1 year

Birth Doula Certifications: Childbirth International

Postpartum Doula Certifications: Childbirth International

Type of Practice: Solo practice

Clients per Month: 1 to 3 births and 1 to 3 postpartum families

Postpartum limits/restrictions: No Smoking

Attends hospital births? All Hospitals

Attends birth center births? All Birth Centers

Attends home births? Any Home Birth

Breastfeeding Certifications: I do not hold a breastfeeding certification but I have breastfed 8 of my own children. All with different issues and I have helped many friends.

Other Relevant Certifications: Child Birth International Birthing Assistant

Specialized training or previous experience: Teens, VBAC, Home Birth, Clients on bed rest, Prematurity, Postpartum mood disorders, Cesarean birth, Induced and high medical technology labor & birth, High risk, Special needs babies, Single parents

Special Services Offered: Cloth diapering education, Babywearing education, Baby-led weaning, Sibling support (for your older children at the birth), Miscarriage Support (Before, During or After), Prenatal or postnatal massage, Reduced rates for military members/spouses, Pregnancy, birth and/or newborn photography, Antepartum doula

Relevant volunteer or advocacy work: I have 8 children. My first two were natural. The other 6 were c-sections. I have also had 4 NAIT babies (Neonatal Alloimmune Thrombocytopenia) that were premature and in the NICU. I want to help comfort women in those situations because I know how terrifying it can be.

Languages spoken: English
