What is a doula?

A birth doula supports you and your partner by providing the knowledge, comfort, and education necessary for a fulfilling birth experience, using techniques such as:

  • Massage
  • Breathing Techniques and Vocalization
  • Counterpressure
  • Visualization
  • Relaxation
  • Education and Advocacy

Doula support allows the laboring woman's partner a greater sense of how to help the laboring mother physically and emotionally. Therefore doula support can help the partner adjust to welcoming a new addition to the family.
